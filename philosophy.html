Our browser extension allows you to opt-in for an anonymized collection of your YouTube recommendations, to support public-interest research. Indeed, despite the huge influence of YouTube’s recommender system, it is totally opaque: YouTube does not provide any data to describe how it works.

The only way we can investigate YouTube, and expose the behavior of its algorithms, is with your help. By sharing your data, you also allow us to detect when the content creators you are subscribed to are being shadow banned, so we can collect evidences on this hidden censorship.
